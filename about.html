About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Arizona (Tucson, AZ), working at the Department of Astronomy and Steward Observatory. I received my PhD from the University of Groningen, where I worked on the early stages of galaxy evolution with space-based observatories such as JWST and HST.

My research focuses on understanding the formation of galaxies in the early Universe, using imaging and spectroscopy from JWST, and studying extreme sources such as Little Red Dots (LRDs).
